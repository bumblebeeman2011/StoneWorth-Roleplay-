Server Information

Welcome to the official StoneWorth Roleplay community and thank you for taking the time to visit and review our server. We are a highly structured and strictly managed Flashing Lights roleplay community focused on realism, teamwork, and professionalism across all departments. We host daily USA East servers with up to 20 players. Our staff ensures moderation, fairness, and stability. We use Google Docs for rules, ranks, and promotions. We strictly prohibit discrimination of any kind. Reporting systems are available. Participation is optional. Thank you for being part of StoneWorth Roleplay.
