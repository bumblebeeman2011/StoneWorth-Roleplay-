About StoneWorth Roleplay

Welcome to the official StoneWorth Roleplay community. Thank you for visiting and reviewing our server. We are a strict and structured Flashing Lights roleplay community focused on realism, teamwork, and professionalism.

We host daily servers in the USA East region, typically running at full capacity with up to 20 players. Our community grows quickly due to active members and consistent hosting schedules.

Our staff team ensures moderation, rule enforcement, and server stability are handled efficiently. We use Google Docs to manage ranks, promotions, and rules for full organization.

We aim to provide a safe, fun, and realistic environment for all players. We do not tolerate racism, sexism, transphobia, or any form of discrimination.

Reporting systems are available for both staff and players. Participation is always voluntary, and we appreciate every member of our community.

Thank you for being here — we hope you enjoy your experience at StoneWorth Roleplay.
