Server Information

Welcome to the official StoneWorth Roleplay community and thank you for taking the time to visit and review our server. We are a highly structured and strictly managed Flashing Lights roleplay community focused on realism, teamwork, and professionalism across all departments. We host daily servers in the USA East region with up to 20 active players per session. Our staff team ensures moderation, fairness, and server stability at all times. We use organized Google Docs systems for rules, ranks, and promotions to maintain structure and efficiency. Our goal is to provide a safe, enjoyable, and realistic roleplay experience for all players. We strictly prohibit discrimination of any kind including racism, sexism, transphobia, or harassment. Reporting systems are available for all members. Participation is always optional. Thank you for being part of StoneWorth Roleplay.
