Server Information

Welcome to the official StoneWorth Roleplay community, and thank you for taking the time to learn about our server. We are a well‑organized and carefully managed Flashing Lights roleplay community built on realism, teamwork, and professionalism across all departments. We host daily USA East servers with up to 20 players, supported by staff who maintain fairness, stability, and a positive environment. Our community uses Google Docs for rules, ranks, and promotions, and we uphold a zero‑tolerance policy toward discrimination. Reporting systems are available for any concerns, and participation is always optional. We appreciate your interest in StoneWorth Roleplay and are glad to have you with us.
